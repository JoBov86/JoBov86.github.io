About Me

I started to learn coding seriously at the start of Covid Lockdown when I was furloughed from my job at the time. I really enjoy it and have made some mini projects to help me learn.

I enjoy learning and developing my skills and am really passionate about progressing with this career. When I'm not studying Web Development and code, I like to code games with my partner in our spare time. Recently we have coded minesweeper and othello in python.
